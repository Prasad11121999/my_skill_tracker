E-Commerce Power BI DashBoard

This is a Power BI dashboard designed to provide a comprehensive analysis of an e-commerce platform's sales and customer data. It includes various data visualizations such as:

  • Sales by Division and District: The table provides a breakdown of total sales by geographic region, displaying values by division and district.
  • Best 5 Customers: A bar chart representing the top 5 customers by total spending.
  • Sales Trends: The yearly sales trend over the years, showing how sales have evolved from 2014 to 2020.
  • Sales by Item Name: A pie chart showing the distribution of sales based on different item names.
  • Sales by Transaction Type: A pie chart breaking down sales by different transaction types, showing the percentage share of each.
  • Sales by Item Key: A bar chart displaying the sum of total sales by individual item keys.
  • Sales by Month: A graph showing monthly sales distribution across the years, providing insights into seasonality.
  • Sales by Region: A pie chart showing the total sales by division with color-coded segments for each region.

The dashboard provides valuable insights into sales performance, customer trends, and helps in understanding the geographical distribution of sales. It is an essential tool for decision-making in the e-commerce sector.
